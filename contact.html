Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to our most common support queries regarding micro-loans, security thresholds, and savings goals.

How long does it take to get a micro-loan approved? +

Most consumer micro-loans and SME capital lines are processed dynamically through our app within 10 minutes. Once your basic registration data matches background metrics, tokens disburse straight to your digital wallet immediately.

What identification documents do I need to register? +

To register for an PayPoint wallet account, you only need an active smartphone number, valid government identity metrics (such as a BVN, NIN, or Voter Card depending on regional requirements), and a clear selfie photo for biometric verification.

Are there monthly maintenance fees on checking wallets? +

No, standard accounts feature zero maintenance overheads. PayPoint-to-PayPoint transfers are completely free forever, allowing micro-merchants and individuals to exchange everyday currency blocks efficiently.

How secure is my money with PayPoint Mobile Bank? +

Your institutional assets are fully licensed under sovereign regulations and backed by structural NDIC insurance caps up to statutory limits. Furthermore, all software pipeline operations run on modern, military-grade end-to-end encryption frameworks.

How does the High-Yield Savings Vault work? +

Our automated high-yield vaults allow you to set fixed deposit targets or daily micro-contributions toward specific milestones (like restocking store inventory). You can lock funds for fixed tenures to earn up to a 12% Annual Percentage Yield (APY).
